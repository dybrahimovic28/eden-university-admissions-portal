SCHOOL OF PHARMACY

Bachelor of Pharmacy

Advancing healthcare through pharmaceutical excellence and research.

Programme Overview

The Bachelor of Pharmacy programme is a rigorous 5-year course designed to produce highly skilled pharmacists capable of working in clinical settings, community pharmacies, and the pharmaceutical industry.

Entry Requirements

  • Five (5) O-Level Credits including English Language, Mathematics, and Sciences.
  • A-Level qualifications in Sciences are an added advantage.

Career Opportunities

  • Clinical Pharmacist
  • Regulatory Affairs Specialist
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
