BSc in Accounting and Finance (BSCAF)

Advance your career with our rigorous academic programme.

Programme Overview

The BSc in Accounting and Finance (BSCAF) is meticulously designed to provide a comprehensive understanding in its respective field. Students develop critical thinking and practical skills required for a successful career.

Minimum Entry Requirements

  • Five (5) Grade 12 / O level Credits or better
  • Must include English Language
  • Must include Mathematics
  • Must include Science

Conditional Admission

Applicants who do not fully meet the standard entry requirements may still be considered for conditional admission after review by the admissions team. We encourage all prospective students to submit their applications for assessment.
