SCHOOL OF NURSING & MIDWIFERY

Bachelor of Science in Nursing

Equipping compassionate healthcare leaders with world-class clinical skills.

Programme Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing at Eden University is designed to prepare highly competent professional nurses capable of providing comprehensive healthcare services. The curriculum blends rigorous academic theory with extensive practical clinical experience in top-tier Zambian hospitals.

Entry Requirements

  • Five (5) O-Level Credits including English Language, Mathematics, and Science.
  • A-Level qualifications in Sciences are an added advantage.
  • Valid NRC or Passport for international students.

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse (RN) in public or private hospitals
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist
  • Public Health Administrator
  • Nurse Educator

Key Information

Duration 4 Years
Study Mode Full-Time
Application Fee ZMW 150 (Zambian) | ZMW 600 (International)
