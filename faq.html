Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Eden University.

How do I apply? +
You can apply entirely online through this portal. Simply click the "Apply Online" button, fill in your details, select your programme, and upload your NRC and Grade 12 / O level results.
What are the requirements? +
For degree programmes, applicants should possess five (5) Grade 12 / O level credits or better. This must include English Language, Mathematics, and Science. View our full requirements page for diploma and masters criteria.
Can I apply if I do not meet all the requirements? +
Yes. Applicants who do not fully meet the standard admission requirements may still be considered for conditional admission, depending on their academic background and the programme applied for. Each application is reviewed individually by the admissions team.
What documents are needed? +
You need a scanned copy of your Grade 12 / O level results (or equivalent) and a copy of your National Registration Card (NRC) or Passport.
What academic qualifications are accepted for international applicants? +
International applicants may submit Original Certificate Copies from recognized examination boards such as ECZ, ZIMSEC, Cambridge, Matric, BGCSE, or equivalent national qualifications from their respective countries.
How long does admission take? +
Our standard processing time is less than 6 hours after receiving a complete application.
How much is the application fee? +
The application fee is ZMW 150 for Zambian students and ZMW 600 for international students.
Can I apply from outside Lusaka? +
Yes! Our online admissions portal supports students across all 10 provinces of Zambia and international applicants worldwide.
What programmes are available? +
We offer over 50 accredited programmes across Nursing, Medicine, Pharmacy, Law, Business, and ICT. You can browse our Programmes Database for full details.
