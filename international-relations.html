Study Permit Support & Immigration Assistance

Eden University is fully responsible for facilitating study permit applications for its international students.

This process is centralized to ensure compliance with immigration regulations and to protect students from fraudulent agents and unnecessary delays.

Important Notice

Students are strongly discouraged from using third-party agents. All study permit applications should be processed directly through Eden University.

Key Information

  • Processing begins immediately upon a student's arrival alongside the registration process.
  • Please refer to your admission letter for arrival instructions.
  • All required documents are submitted through the University to the Department of Immigration.
  • Medical checkups are conducted on campus to meet immigration requirements.
  • The University facilitates timely processing of study permits for eligible students.

Required Documents

  • Certified copy of Passport Bio-Data Page
  • Eden University Acceptance Letter
  • Medical Report (Conducted at the University)
  • Passport-Sized Photographs
