Application Processing Fee

A non-refundable application processing fee is required before your application can be processed.

  • Zambian Applicants: ZMW 150
  • International Applicants: ZMW 600

The purpose of this fee is to confirm the seriousness of an application and begin official admissions processing.

After payment, the Admissions Team will guide applicants through the next steps.

Important Payment Information

The Application Processing Fee is paid first. All other fees including:

  • Tuition Fees
  • Student Permit Fees
  • Registration Fees
  • Accommodation Fees

are normally paid after arrival at Eden University and during registration.

For payment guidance, students should contact the Admissions Team.

Frequently Asked Questions About Fees

Official policies and answers regarding tuition, accommodation, and payments.

🔍
Q: Is the application fee refundable? +

No. The application fee is completely non-refundable.

The fee confirms the seriousness of an application and allows the Admissions Team to begin processing documents and reviewing eligibility.

Q: How does accommodation work? +

Students may choose:

  • On-Campus Accommodation – ZMW 5,500 per semester
  • Private Boarding Houses – typically ZMW 800 to ZMW 2,000 per month depending on facilities and location.
Q: What payment methods are accepted? +

International Students:

  • Mukuru
  • WorldRemit

Zambian Students:

  • Airtel Money
  • MTN Money
  • Zamtel Money

Payment details are provided by the Admissions Office.

Admissions WhatsApp: +260779934886

Q: What does the fee calculator estimate? +

The calculator provides an estimated total cost of completing a programme based on tuition, accommodation and other applicable fees.

It helps parents and students plan financially for the entire duration of study.

Q: Can I stay in a private boarding house? +

Yes.

Students may stay in approved private boarding houses according to their budget and preferences.

Q: How do student permits work? +

After receiving an Acceptance Letter, international students should print the letter and travel with it.

Upon entering Zambia, students are normally granted time to complete registration.

The Student Permit application is then processed through Eden University with assistance from the Admissions Team.

The Admissions Team guides students through the entire permit process.
