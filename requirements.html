Admission Requirements

Ensure you meet our academic standards before submitting your application.

Degree Programmes (Undergraduate)

Minimum Entry Requirements

Applicants should possess:

  • Five (5) Grade 12 / O-Level Credits or better
  • Must include English Language
  • Must include Mathematics
  • Must include Science
Note: A-Level qualifications are considered an added advantage and may grant exemptions.

Conditional Admission

Applicants who do not fully meet the minimum programme requirements may still be considered for conditional admission, subject to review and approval by the admissions team.

Eden University evaluates each application individually. Applicants who may be missing certain subject requirements or have qualifications that do not fully meet the standard entry criteria are encouraged to apply, as conditional admission opportunities may be available.

Notice: Meeting the minimum requirements does not automatically guarantee admission. All applications are subject to review by the admissions team.

Diploma Applicants

Ideal for students seeking practical, focused qualifications.

  • Minimum of Three (3) to Four (4) Grade 12 / O-Level Credits
  • English Language is mandatory

Masters Applicants

For advanced postgraduate studies and research.

  • A recognized Bachelor's Degree in a related field
  • Updated Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Two academic/professional references

International Students

We welcome students from all over the world.

Required Academic Documents

International applicants must provide:

  • Original Certificate Copies of high school qualifications from their country's recognized examination board
  • Passport Copy
  • Any additional supporting academic documents (if applicable)

Examples of accepted examination boards include:

  • ECZ
  • ZIMSEC
  • Cambridge
  • Matric
  • BGCSE

and other equivalent national qualifications. Study Permit assistance provided upon admission.
