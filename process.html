The Application Process

A simple, transparent 6-step journey from application to enrollment.

1. Choose Programme

Explore our programmes database and select the course that aligns with your career goals.

2. Complete Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.

3. Upload Documents

Attach your NRC/Passport and Grade 12 / O level results (or equivalent transcripts).

4. Application Review

Our admissions committee reviews your submission. This typically takes less than 6 hours.

5. Admission Decision

If successful, you will receive an official Offer Letter via email and WhatsApp.

6. Registration

Pay your required fees and register for your first semester classes.

Required Documents

  • Scanned copy of Grade 12 / O level Results (or equivalent)
  • Scanned copy of NRC or Passport
  • Proof of Application Fee payment (if requested)

Processing Timelines

Standard Processing: less than 6 Hours
Next Intake: January & July
